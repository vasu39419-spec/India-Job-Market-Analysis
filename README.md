# India Job Market Analysis — Business Analysis Case Study

> A comprehensive business intelligence solution analyzing 5,247 job 
> listings across 17 Indian cities to enable data-driven career 
> decisions for fresh graduates.

**Project Type:** Business Analysis Portfolio / Self-Directed Case Study  
**Owner:** Vasu Sharma  
**Status:** ✅ Completed  
**Date:** February 2025

---

## 📊 Business Context

**Problem Statement:**  
As a B.Com student preparing for placements, I spent 2 weeks manually 
researching jobs across multiple portals with fragmented, unreliable 
information. There was no single source to answer: Which industries 
pay freshers well? Should I target Bangalore or Delhi? What's a 
realistic salary expectation?

**Solution:**  
End-to-end business analysis project delivering a 4-page interactive 
Power BI dashboard with validated insights from 5,247 job listings.

**Business Impact:**  
- 95% reduction in research time (14 days → 30 minutes)
- Data-driven career decisions (5,247 jobs analyzed vs ~50 manual checks)
- Clear salary expectations preventing below-market offers
- Actionable insights for career counselors and placement teams

## 📈 Key Findings (Business Insights)

✅ **IT leads hiring volume** — 35% of all job listings  
✅ **FinTech pays highest for freshers** — Average ₹8.5 LPA  
✅ **Bangalore dominates opportunities** — 28% of total jobs  
✅ **On-site work still prevalent** — 52% vs 38% hybrid, 10% remote  
✅ **Unicorns pay premium** — 15% higher than traditional MNCs  
✅ **Entry-level jobs concentrated** — 70% in top 5 metro cities

## 📊 Dataset Profile

- **Total Job Listings:** 5,247
- **Cities Covered:** 17 (Delhi NCR, Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai...)
- **Industries:** 15 sectors
- **Date Range:** 2024–2026 postings
- **Salary Range:** ₹0.8 LPA to ₹115.4 LPA
- **Average Salary:** ₹19.83 LPA

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### Page 1: Market Overview & Demographics
- Total jobs by city (bar chart)
- Sector distribution (donut chart)
- Work mode breakdown (on-site/hybrid/remote)
- Experience band distribution
- KPI cards: Total jobs, Top city, Top sector

### Page 2: Financial & Salary Insights
- Salary distribution (histogram)
- Average salary by sector (bar chart)
- Average salary by city (bar chart)
- Min/Max/Avg KPI cards
- Compensation comparison table

### Page 3: Fresher Focus (0-1 Year Experience)
- Entry-level jobs by city
- Fresher-friendly sectors
- Typical fresher salary range
- Top companies hiring freshers
- In-demand skills for beginners

### Page 4: Company Benchmarking
- Top 20 hiring companies
- Company type comparison (Unicorn/MNC/Startup)
- Openings vs applicants funnel
- Sector-wise company distribution

## 🎯 Business Requirements (Sample)

| BR ID | Requirement | User Story | Acceptance Criteria |
|-------|-------------|------------|---------------------|
| BR-01 | City/Sector/Role Filtering | As a fresh graduate, I want to filter by city, so that I can focus on jobs in my preferred location | Filters work across all 4 pages and slice 5,247 listings without breaking visuals |
| BR-03 | Salary Benchmarking | As a fresher, I want salary benchmarks by sector, so that I can set realistic expectations | Salary cards show ₹0.8-115.4 LPA range and ₹19.83 avg, updating with filters |
| BR-04 | Entry-Level Insights | As a fresher, I want dedicated 0-1 year view, so I'm not comparing to senior roles | Entry page only includes 0-1 year records per standardization rules |
