# India Job Market Analysis Dashboard (2024-2026) 💼📊

An interactive 4-page Power BI dashboard providing macroeconomic insights, salary breakdowns, company benchmarking, and targeted entry-level dynamics across the Indian employment landscape.

## 🛠️ Dashboard Architecture & Structure

### 1. Market Overview & Demographics
* **Macro Footprint:** Tracks a comprehensive database of **5,000 active job listings** across **17 major Indian cities**.
* **Sector Dominance:** Showcases market sector sizing, identifying *Information Technology* as the leading employer volume source (1,545 jobs), followed by *FinTech* (543) and *E-Commerce* (511).
* **Workplace Preferences:** Evaluates operational settings showing *On-Site* configurations leading at 40.1%, closely matched with *Hybrid* tracks at 34.5%.

### 2. Financial & Salary Insights
* **Earning Ranges:** Maps a wide compensation curve stretching from a baseline floor of **0.80 LPA up to a peak of 115.40 LPA** (Average Market Salary: **19.83 LPA**).
* **Sector Premiums:** Isolates *Logistics* (24 LPA) and *Retail* (22 LPA) as unexpected premium average salary niches over standard technical paths.
* **Role Benchmarks:** Compares key functional paths, identifying *Data Scientists* at the top tier tiering index (24 LPA avg) over *Data Analysts* (21 LPA avg).

### 3. Entry-Level & Fresher Dynamics
* **Isolated Slicing:** A dedicated drill-down dashboard tracking active entry-level indicators (0-1 year experience).
* **Niche Breakdown:** Pinpoints targeted geographic pockets, highlighting structured hubs like *Ahmedabad* alongside tech-heavy entry profiles like *Node.js Developers* (averaging 8.6 LPA in rookie brackets).

### 4. Enterprise & Corporate Benchmarking
* **Funnel Scaling:** Tracks the balance between volume demands (**18K aggregate openings**) against structural scale requirements (**2M applicants**) across core sectors.
* **Tier Tiers:** Contrasts corporate funding models, proving *Indian Unicorns* offer premium starting scales (25 LPA average) vs. standard *MNCs* (18 LPA).
* **Volume Leaders:** Identifies market liquidity sources led actively by fintech operators like *Fi Money* (101 active listings) and *Slice* (89 listings).

---

## 📈 Key Insights Discovered
1. **The Unicorn Premium:** Tech startups designated as Indian Unicorns outpay established legacy MNCs by an average margin of **~38%** to attract top tier engineering profiles.
2. **Experience Concentration:** The core of current marketplace acquisition targets the *Mid-Level (3-6 years)* segment, holding the single largest talent requisition block at 1,378 listings.
3. **Data Role Inversion:** Advanced quantitative specialties (*Data Science*) command clear 14% base average premium jumps over generalized descriptive tracks (*Data Analytics*).

---

## ⚙️ Core Technical Stack Used
* **Platform Application:** Microsoft Power BI Desktop
* **Engine Foundations:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for calculated parameters & metrics formatting
* **Visual Objects:** Donut visual matrices, custom cards containers, decomposed clustered horizontal bar hierarchies, and granular cross-filtering visual slicers.
